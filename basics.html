Video Games Are Pretty Cool

First, we need to know what a video game is

Video Games

A video game is a a game played by electronically manipulating images produced by a computer program on a television screen or other display screen. (According to Oxford Languages Dictionary)

Types Of Games

Games have never been limited to just one typ. Here are a few popular genres

  • RPG: Stands for "role playing game" is a game type centered around playing as a set character traveling through a fantasy setting
  • MMO: Stands for "Mass Multiplayer Online Game" is a type of game where you play on huge online serever with ither real people and cooperate to accomplish a given quest or to obtain an item
  • FPS: Stands for "First Person Shooter" is a type of game where you play looking through the eyes of your character and you wield a weapon (typically a firearm) in order to take down enemies

Below are the world's most popular games

  1. Tetris: a classic game created in Russia during teh Soviet Union
  2. Minecraft: an open-world game where you build what you want in a 3D cube-based world
  3. Grand Theft Auto V: a mature game set in the fictional city of San Andreas where you play as criminals attempting their biggest heist to date
  4. Wii Sports: a game for the classic Nintendo Wii which is meant to emulate real life sports by making use of the Wii's motion tracking controllers
